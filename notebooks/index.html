Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services.

Do I need to book an appointment?
Yes, we recommend booking an appointment in advance to ensure you get your preferred time slot. You can book online or call us directly.
What products do you use?
We use only premium, salon-grade products from trusted brands that are safe for all hair and skin types.
How long does a typical service take?
Service times vary - haircuts take 30-60 minutes, coloring 2-4 hours, facials 60-90 minutes. We'll confirm timing when you book.
Do you offer bridal packages?
Yes! We offer comprehensive bridal packages including trial sessions, makeup, hair styling, and nail services. Contact us for custom quotes.
What is your cancellation policy?
Please notify us at least 24 hours in advance for cancellations or rescheduling to avoid any charges.
